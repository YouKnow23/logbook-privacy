Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 15, 2025

Logbook is a personal workout tracking application designed to help users create workout templates and log training sessions. Your privacy is important, and this app is built to function without collecting personal data.

Data Collection

Logbook does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data.

The app does not require account creation and does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Storage

All workout-related data, including templates, exercises, and workout history, is stored locally on your device only.

This data never leaves your device. Deleting the app will permanently remove all stored data.

Third-Party Services

Logbook does not integrate with third-party services that collect user data.

Children’s Privacy

Logbook does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.
